Rock Climbing Videos

Rock climbing is my favorite activity. It's what I spent most of my free time doing. One of the most intriguing aspects of climbing is the problem solving. (Starting to see a theme here?) Not only does climbing require physical strength, mental toughness, and flawless technique, but it's a puzzle for your body. It's up to you to determine the best and most efficient way to move through a sequence of moves.

I have included some of my favorite videos below. I hope they highlight the many appealing aspects of climbing, whether that's being with friends and family, grit and perseverance, or just being out in nature or being a part of something bigger than yourself.
